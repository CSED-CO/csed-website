Educator Preview

Tuesday, September 29th | Google Boulder

Encouraging the next generation of designers, developers, engineers & innovators.

WHAT is the Educator Preview?

It’s a special sneak peak of Boulder’s Computer Science Education Week activities. We’ll show you ways we can bring computer science into your classroom with lessons that fit into your existing curriculum and standards, no matter what subject or grade you teach.

WHY should I go?

Computer science gives us new ways of approaching problems in any lesson or classroom, and we want to show you how it can help you and your class do more with new approaches. You’ll get a chance for one-on-one time with local industry professionals and programming enthusiasts, as well as have plenty of time for questions and hands-on experimentation and play with what we have to offer. Let us help you prepare now for CSED Week in December!
